Posting on instagram

The diagram shows how posting on instagram works. You upload picture and then your device sends data through isp. instagram stores data in cloud storage.

uploading your image

  • step 1 connect your device to the internet
  • step 2 open instagram and select create post icon
  • step 3 either take a picture with the app or upload a picture from your file
  • step 4 upload your picture

The data travels

After you upload your picture your device sends your data through your isp.

saving your files through the cloud

The isproutes your data to instagrams server storage where it is stored in the database and the cloud

notification

instagram servers sends a notification to your followers that you have made a post

viewing

the server sends data packets to the veiwers device and it reassembles it into your post
